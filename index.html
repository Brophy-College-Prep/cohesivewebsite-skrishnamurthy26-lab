Seb never goes to the gym because he’s “bulking,” but the only thing getting bigger is his list of excuses.

He heard there’s something called leg day and decided that was a red flag. His favorite gym machine is the vending machine, and his cardio routine is running late, running his mouth, and running from responsibilities.

He’s on a strict program: three sets of “I’ll start Monday” with unlimited rest days. Every time someone says “just do one more rep,” Seb disappears like it’s a magic trick. He goes to the gym religiously: once every few months, just to keep the streak alive.
