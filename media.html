This page has a YouTube video that tours the LA fitness gym .

This LA Fitness location has a bright, open layout with rows of cardio machines, a large free-weight area, and plenty of strength equipment for full-body workouts. There are spaces for stretching and functional training, plus courts and group fitness studios where people can take classes or play sports. The gym is designed so you can do almost any type of workout in one place, whether you want to lift, run, swim, or just get in a quick session before or after school.
